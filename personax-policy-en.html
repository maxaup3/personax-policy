Policy Overview

PersonaX is committed to providing a safe and healthy social creative platform for users. This policy explains how we protect the safety of users aged 13 and above, particularly minors, and how we comply with applicable laws and regulations, including but not limited to the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the Digital Services Act (DSA).

Age Requirements and User Protection

Recommended Age

  • Minimum Age: 13 years and older
  • Reason: The app involves social interaction, content creation, and sharing features that require users to have basic internet safety awareness

Minor User Protection

We pay special attention to the safety of minor users and implement the following measures:

  • Age verification at account registration: Users must declare their age during registration, and users under 13 will be refused registration
  • Account deletion rights: Minor users may request to delete their account and related data at any time
  • Parent contact: Parents may contact us if they discover misuse

Data Collection and Privacy Protection

Information We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect the following personal information:

  • ✗ Precise location data (GPS, geolocation)
  • ✗ Biometric information
  • ✗ Device identifiers (except for account security purposes)

Minimized Information Collection

To provide our services, we only collect:

  • Account information (username, email, password hash)
  • User-generated content (posts, images, comments)
  • Basic interaction data (likes, comment counts)
  • Device type and OS version (for compatibility and crash reporting)

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or data brokers. All user data is used only to improve app features and is not sold to third parties.

Social Interaction and Content Moderation

Social Features

Currently available features:

  • ✓ Post publishing and commenting
  • ✓ Likes and following
  • ✓ @mentions of other users
  • ✓ Tags and keyword search

Currently unavailable features:

  • ✗ Direct messages (planned for future release with additional safety measures)

Content Moderation and Reporting

User Reporting Mechanism

  • Users can report inappropriate content (harassment, hate speech, misinformation, etc.)
  • Reporting process: Open post details → Click "Report" → Select reason → Submit
  • Processing time: We commit to reviewing reported content within 24-48 hours
  • If violation is confirmed, the content will be deleted and/or the account will be disabled

AI-Generated Content Moderation

  • All AI-generated images must pass human review before publication
  • Review standards:
    • ✓ Allowed: Normal character depictions, creative scenes, artistic expression
    • ✗ Prohibited: Inappropriate content, violence, hate speech, explicit content, child-related inappropriate content
  • Violation found: Violating content will be immediately deleted, and users will receive a warning or account suspension

Violation Handling

Violation Type Action Taken
Minor violation (first offense) Content removal + user warning
Moderate violation Content removal + account warning + temporary posting restriction
Severe violation (child safety concern) Permanent content deletion + account suspension + record preservation
Multiple violations Permanent account suspension

Account Management and User Rights

Account Control

Users have complete control over their accounts:

Account Deletion

  • Users can request to delete their account from settings at any time
  • After deletion, personal information and content will be completely removed within 30 days
  • Deleted accounts cannot be recovered

Content Deletion

  • Users can delete any content they have posted (posts, comments, images)
  • Deletion takes effect immediately

Privacy Settings

  • Users can control whether their profile is public
  • Users can choose to block specific users from following or interacting

Special Rights for Minors

  • When minor users delete their account, the system will request confirmation and preserve evidence for future complaints
  • Before account deletion, users can download all their data

Data Security and Protection

Security Measures

  • Password Protection: Passwords are stored using encryption algorithms (bcrypt) and cannot be reverse-decrypted; users should not share their passwords
  • Transmission Security: All communication uses HTTPS encryption; API requests use JWT token authentication
  • Data Access Control: Only authorized employees can access user data (for moderation and technical support), and all access is logged
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security reviews and vulnerability testing; issues are fixed immediately when discovered

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as the account exists
  • Deleted accounts: Data is completely deleted within 30 days
  • Report records: For safety reasons, report data is retained for a maximum of 1 year

Account Suspension and Violations

Account Suspension Reasons

Accounts may be suspended for:

  • Multiple instances of posting inappropriate content
  • Harassing, threatening, or bullying other users
  • Violations concerning child safety
  • Repeatedly ignoring community guidelines

Appeal Process

Suspended users can:

  1. Email personaX@echo.tech to explain their situation
  2. Provide evidence of account behavior before suspension
  3. We will review and respond within 7 business days

Parent and Guardian Guidelines

Recommendations for Parents

  1. Communication: Discuss how to use the app and internet safety; explain what constitutes inappropriate content and how to report it
  2. Account Supervision: Regularly review your child's posts and interactions; encourage them to share all interactions with strangers
  3. Privacy Settings: Help your child configure privacy settings to limit access from strangers

Parent Complaints and Assistance

If parents discover any content or behavior that endangers their child's safety:

  1. Direct Report: Use the in-app reporting feature to report inappropriate content
  2. Contact Us: Use the "Contact Developer" feature within Google Play to send us an email
  3. Request Deletion: We commit to taking action within 24 hours of confirming the issue

Legal Compliance

COPPA Compliance

PersonaX complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • ✓ Does not provide service to users under 13
  • ✓ Does not collect personal information from users under 13
  • ✓ Does not sell user data to third parties
  • ✓ Users and parents may request to view or delete data at any time

GDPR Compliance

For EU users, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • ✓ Users have the right to access, correct, and delete their data
  • ✓ We collect and process data only as necessary
  • ✓ Users can withdraw consent at any time
  • ✓ In case of data breach, we will report to authorities and users within 72 hours

DSA Compliance

EU Digital Services Act requirements:

  • ✓ Provide transparent content moderation policy
  • ✓ Users can report inappropriate content
  • ✓ Users have appeal rights
  • ✓ Publish transparency reports regularly

Contact Us

Child Safety Issues

Response time: 24-48 hours (business days)

For child safety related issues, please use the "Contact Us" feature in the Google Play Store to send an email to the PersonaX developer. We will respond within 24-48 hours.

Appendix

A. Reportable Content Types

Users can report the following content:

  • Harassment, threats, or bullying
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • False or misleading information
  • Spam
  • Copyright infringement
  • Violence or illegal content
  • Child-related inappropriate content
  • Other content violating community guidelines

B. Community Guidelines Summary

All users agree to:

  1. Respect others' privacy and rights
  2. Not post harassing, hate speech, or violent content
  3. Not impersonate others or commit fraud
  4. Comply with applicable laws
  5. Not disrupt the normal operation of the app

Users who violate guidelines may be warned, restricted, or suspended.

C. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. When we make major changes, we will:

  1. Notify users through in-app notifications
  2. Request user confirmation of the new policy
  3. Maintain previous versions for reference
